What We Do

Our Focus Areas

We focus on activities that contribute to the successful functioning of the U.S. labor exchange — serving federal, state, local, and private workforce systems.

🤖
Automated Coding Solutions

Advanced auto-coding applications including the O*NET-SOC AutoCoder to assign occupation codes to job orders, resumes, applicants, and LMI data with speed and accuracy.

🔍
Next-Generation Job Search

State-of-the-art job search technology including Enigma AJS — purpose-built for the modern workforce and designed to connect workers with opportunities at scale.

📊
Real-Time Labor Market Information

Live, actionable labor market data and analytics that help public and private workforce systems make informed decisions about employment trends and workforce needs.

🗂️
Taxonomy Development & Linkages

Expert development, refinement, and linkage of occupational taxonomies — ensuring your systems speak a common language across all workforce information platforms.

🔬
Pure & Applied Research

Rigorous research initiatives that advance the science of workforce systems — from foundational studies to practical applied research solving today's employment challenges.

🖥️
Workforce Information Systems

End-to-end development of visionary workforce information systems, plus design and usability testing of application interfaces that are intuitive for every user.
